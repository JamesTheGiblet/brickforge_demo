About Relief Studio

This tool creates 3D relief models by converting image brightness into physical height:

  • Each pixel becomes a stack of 1x1 plates
  • Brightness determines stack height (darker = taller when inverted)
  • Color matching to available LEGO bricks
  • Generate step-by-step layer instructions
  • Export complete parts list for ordering

Perfect for creating topographic maps, company logos, portraits, and decorative wall art with depth and texture.

Tip: For text and logos, enable "Invert Depth" to make dark areas (text) stand out.
